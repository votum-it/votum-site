1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit our website. They help us remember your preferences, understand how you use our site, and improve your experience.

4. Cookie Duration

  • Session cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies: Remain on your device for a set period (typically 30 days to 2 years)

You can see the specific expiration dates in your browser's cookie settings.

5. Your Cookie Choices

How to Control Cookies

In your browser:

  1. Go to your browser settings/preferences
  2. Find "Cookies" or "Privacy" section
  3. Choose to accept, reject, or manage cookies
  4. Some browsers allow you to set specific rules by website

Browser guides:

  • Chrome
  • Firefox
  • Safari
  • Edge

Cookie Consent Management

When you first visit our website, you'll see a cookie consent banner. You can:

  • Accept all: Enable all non-essential cookies
  • Reject all: Disable all non-essential cookies
  • Customize: Choose which types of cookies to allow

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clicking the cookie settings link in the footer.

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable cookies:

  • Essential functions will still work (site will remain functional)
  • Analytics will be limited (we won't see how you use the site)
  • Personalization will be reduced (saved preferences won't work)
  • Some features may not work optimally (forms, login, etc.)

7. Do Not Track (DNT)

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. We respect your DNT preference, though many websites don't currently honor it. When we detect DNT enabled, we disable analytics and marketing cookies.

8. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Delete personal information collected from you
  • Opt-out of the "sale" of personal information

We do not sell your personal information. For CCPA requests, contact: privacy@votum.com

9. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU, we require your consent before enabling non-essential cookies. You can:

  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Exercise other rights under GDPR

Contact: privacy@votum.com
